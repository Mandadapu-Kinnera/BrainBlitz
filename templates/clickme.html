Part of Speech Definition Example
Noun A word that names a person, place, thing, or idea. John, cat, happiness
Pronoun A word that replaces a noun. he, she, it
Adjective Describes a noun or pronoun. red, happy, tall
Verb Shows an action or state of being. run, jump, is
Adverb Modifies a verb, adjective, or adverb. quickly, very, now
Preposition Shows the relationship of a noun or pronoun to another word. on, in, at
Conjunction Connects words, phrases, or clauses. and, but, because
Interjection Expresses emotion or feeling. Wow!, Ouch!, Oh no!
